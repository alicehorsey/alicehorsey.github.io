The photos below show a snapshot of my ten years in the Theatre and Live Events industry! As a stage manager, creative problem solving, excellent communication, analytical thinking and organisation, responding to change, managing people and lots of quick thinking in live situations (to ensure the show carries on, no matter what is happening behind the scenes!) are all part of daily life.

It was challenging in ways I could not have imagined and more rewarding than I had ever hoped. It was FUN!
